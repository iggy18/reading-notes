# Comparison operators: Evaluating.

## need to know!

- **==** equal to
- **!=** not equal to
- **===** strict equal to
- **!==** strict not equal to
- **>** greater than
- **<** less than
- **>** = greater than or equal to
- **<=** less than or equal to

###logical operators
- **&&** logical and
- **||** logical or
- **!** logical not
